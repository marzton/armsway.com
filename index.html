Frequently Asked Questions

What sizes are available?

ArmsWay™ barrier sleeves are designed to fit standard adult blood pressure cuffs. Large/XL sizes are available — contact us for full sizing specifications.

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

We accept orders of any size, but case-quantity pricing begins at 100 units. Volume discount pricing is available for orders of 500+ units. Contact us for a custom quote.

How long does shipping take?

Standard orders ship within 2–5 business days from our Florida facility. Expedited shipping is available. Bulk/pallet orders may require additional lead time.

Are these products FDA cleared?

ArmsWay™ barrier sleeves are a Class I exempt medical device. Please contact us for current regulatory documentation to support your procurement process.

Can we get private-label or custom packaging?

Yes — private-label and custom packaging options are available for qualifying distribution partners. Contact us to discuss requirements and lead times.

Who are your current distributors?

ArmsWay™ products are distributed through Hopkins Medical Products and Dynarex Corporation. Direct purchasing is also available. Find a distributor near you.
